# Prompt de Vídeo - AUTistima (30 Segundos)

Este documento contém um prompt detalhado para gerar um vídeo comercial de 30 segundos usando ferramentas de IA Generativa (como Sora, Runway, Kling, Luma), focado em demonstrar as funcionalidades do aplicativo AUTistima.

## Prompt para IA de Vídeo (Inglês)

> **Prompt:**
> A high-quality, cinematic 30-second commercial for a mobile app called "AUTistima".
>
> **Visual Style:** Clean, modern UI, soft lighting, warm colors (Salmon #F28B82 and Light Blue #AECBFA). Professional but welcoming atmosphere. High resolution, photorealistic lifestyle shots mixed with app interface close-ups.
>
> **Scene 1 (0s-5s):** A mother looks concerned at a pile of papers on a table. She picks up her phone. Close-up on the phone screen opening the "AUTistima" app. The logo animates gently. The tagline "Política pública em forma de afeto" fades in.
>
> **Scene 2 (5s-10s):** Cut to the app interface showing a map with pins. A finger taps a pin labeled "Escola Inclusiva". A card slides up showing school details and a "Verified" badge. The mother nods, looking impressed.
>
> **Scene 3 (10s-15s):** Transition to the "Meus Filhos" (My Children) screen. We see a profile of a child with a photo and a "Carteirinha" (ID card) icon. The user taps "Manejos" (Management) and logs a daily activity quickly. It looks organized and simple.
>
> **Scene 4 (15s-20s):** The screen changes to the "Chat" or "Acolhimento" section. A message bubble pops up: "Grupo de Mães: Alguém indica neuropediatra?". The user types a reply. A sense of community and connection.
>
> **Scene 5 (20s-25s):** Split screen showing a "Profissionais" search result list on the left and the mother smiling with relief on the right. She books an appointment with a tap.
>
> **Scene 6 (25s-30s):** The mother puts the phone down, looking relaxed and happy, hugging her child. The final shot is the AUTistima logo on a clean white background with the text "Baixe agora" and App Store/Google Play icons.
